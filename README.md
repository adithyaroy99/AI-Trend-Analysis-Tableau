# AI Trend Analysis (Tableau) — Adoption, VC Investment & AI Readiness

Two interactive Tableau dashboards that compare **AI adoption (2021–2025)**, **AI-focused VC investment (2012–2025)**, and **Government AI Readiness (2023)** to highlight country-level gaps and prioritise opportunities.

## What this shows (in 10 seconds)
- Where AI adoption is rising/falling across Europe over time  
- How VC investment concentration compares across countries  
- Which countries look “high opportunity” when you combine adoption + readiness + investment signals (2023)

## Dashboards
- **AI Adoption & Investment Overview (2021–2025):** trend and ranking views for adoption rate vs investment distribution  
- **Opportunity & Prioritisation (2023):** quadrant-style segmentation using thresholds and a weighted priority score

## Data sources
- Eurostat — AI adoption (2021–2025)  
- OECD — AI VC investment (2012–2025)  
- Oxford Insights — Government AI Readiness Index (2023)
