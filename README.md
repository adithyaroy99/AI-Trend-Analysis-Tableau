# AI Trend Analysis (Tableau) — Adoption, VC Investment & Readiness

Interactive Tableau dashboards that compare AI adoption trends (2021–2025), AI-focused VC investment concentration, and AI readiness (2023) to highlight country-level opportunities and prioritisation.

## Dashboards
- **AI Adoption & Investment Overview (2021–2025):** trend + ranking views to compare adoption rates and investment distribution.
- **Opportunity & Prioritisation (2023):** country segmentation using a weighted priority score and threshold-based quadrants.

## Data sources
- Eurostat — AI adoption (2021–2025)
- OECD — AI VC investment (2012–2025)
- Oxford Insights — Government AI Readiness Index (2023)

## Files in this repo
- `AI Trends.twb` — Tableau workbook
- `AI Trend Analysis.pdf` — project report
